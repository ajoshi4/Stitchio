The Problem

For many hands on skills like stitching, it is much more difficult to learn without the proper guidance of the techniques and tools. Especially when you're just starting out and you're looking for more affordable options. Even learning how to use a sewing machine or what settings to work with can be difficult.

Pain Points

  • Difficult to learn hands on skills on your own without guidance
  • Knowing what tools to use with what materials
  • Finding the right practice plan catered to your goals
  • Finding affordable m,aterials for training and practice
